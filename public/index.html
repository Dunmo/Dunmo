Meet Dunmo!

This is Dunmo! He wants to save you time and make your life easier by managing your time for you. Until now, you had to figure out when to do each of your tasks. It was your job to find time in your day to do them. Now you can relax and let Dunmo do the work for you.

How can Dunmo make my life easier?

Dunmo prioritizes your task list and schedules each task into your calendar so you always know what you should be working on. This way you can see clearly what you need to do, when you need to do it, and how long you'll need to spend on it. With Dunmo doing the work for you, you'll have more time to spend doing the things you love.
