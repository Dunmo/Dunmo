Everything in one place

(show layout without tasks)
Tasks and projects, work and personal, team and individual. It's all stuff you have to do so why not keep it all in one place?










Focus on now

(show agenda w/ a few tasks)
With Dunmo prioritizing your task list and helping you manage your time, you can spend more time in the now, getting things done. Your agenda will be your main view to track what you're working on now and see what's next up on the list.





Easy task adding

(show adding task)
Tasks are hard, adding them to a productivity app shouldn't feel like a chore. I don't know how we'll make it easy with this design but we need to.

Filter for focus

(show example filtered agenda)
Add #tags to any task and you'll be able to filter any view by that tag. This helps avoid the overwhelming feeling of a long list as well as avoid getting distracted letting your mind wander on to an unrelated task from your list.

Project management made easy

(show project page)
Project management doesn't have to be complicated. It's essentially a #tag on the task, but you can manage teams.
